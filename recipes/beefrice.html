Beef Rice

Introduction

For those who come from central Kenya, they well understand why beef rice is an ideal meal.

Having massive rice farms it's just irresistible not to occasionally prepare beef rice.

Here, I'l take your through some cool steps of how to pull out the best beef riceeee!!!!!!!!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Rice
  • 0.25 boiled meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Coooking oil
  • Garlic and ginger mix
  • Tumeric
  • Red Onions and Spring Onions
  • Black and sweet pepper
  • Water and Salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1
    2. In a cooking pot with oil,go in with your onions. cook until soft then add in your tomatoes mix well,add in your black pepper,garlic & ginger mix and tumeric then give it a good mix and leave it to cook for abt 2 mins
  2. Step 2
    3. Add in your royco cubes,tomato paste,salt and sweet pepper.
  3. Step 3
    4. Stir and add your boiled meat and let it cool with the pot's content.
  4. Step 4
    5. Add in your rice,mix it up and then add your spring onions on top.
  5. Step 5
    6. Add 4 cups of water...mix...let it boil....cover and let it steam.
  6. Step 6
    7. luff it up once all the water is drained and serve.
  7. Step 7

    8. You can go in with your kachumbari salad or chili masala.
